Building an online presence as a DJ is something I've been putting real effort into lately. 
Social media isn't really my thing personally — never been a Facebook or TikTok guy — but 
when it comes to getting music out there, you work with the tools available.

Here's where you can find me:

- 🎵 [SoundCloud](https://soundcloud.com/aksf) — mixes and tracks
- 📺 [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@tone-1111) — sets and visual content
- 🎮 [Twitch](https://www.twitch.tv/t0ne1111) — live DJ streams
- 🎧 [Kick](https://kick.com/toneonthedecks) — another live stream home

Using [Restream](https://restream.io/about) to push live sessions across multiple platforms 
simultaneously, which has been a game changer for reach without the extra overhead.

On the production side I've been getting into Adobe Audition for crafting DJ drop samples — 
that deserves its own post once I have more to show. Also been using Adobe Express for 
artwork and logo work, which has been fun to learn.
